What is Zemi?

Zemi is a simple BMI calculator that helps you determine your Body Mass Index and provide meal suggestions based on your BMI results. Cut-offs for BMI categories are baseed on NHS guidelines for Black African and Caribbean adults and South Asian adults, which differ from the standard BMI categories.
